Data Science

  • Define and explain key concepts in data science, such as data, variables, and different types of data.
  • Develop proficiency in programming languages commonly used in data science, such as Python or R.
  • Learn techniques for exploring and analyzing data, including data cleaning, visualization, and statistical analysis.
  • Acquire skills in manipulating and preprocessing data to make it suitable for analysis, including handling missing values and outliers.
  • Understand the principles of machine learning and be able to apply various algorithms for tasks such as classification, regression, and clustering.
  • Learn how to evaluate and validate machine learning models to ensure their accuracy and generalizability.
